ROOPREKHA

Line becomes Form. Sketch becomes Structure. Bridging the gap between artistic intuition and parameter-heavy digital manufacturing.

The Bottleneck

The Manual Modeling Trap.

Manual jewellery designers think in sketches—intuitive, aesthetic, unconstrained. CAD designers build in parameters—wall thicknesses, prong tolerances, casting rules.

The handoff is slow, lossy, and entirely dependent on scarce specialists who must mentally translate an artistic impression into manufacturable geometry. We eliminate this friction.

01

Sketch Input

Accepts raw 2D concepts via photo, scan, or digital drawing—exactly what designers already produce.

02

CV Extraction

Advanced deep-tech algorithms map depth, trace curves, and extract precise geometric proportions.

03

Constraint Layer

Automatically enforces real-world manufacturing rules, casting minimums, and stone-seat tolerances.

04

Native Output

Generates production-ready .3dm files perfectly compatible with existing MatrixGold and Rhino workflows.
